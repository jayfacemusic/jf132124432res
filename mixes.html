The story behind Jayface Radio

In 2023, Jayface launched a mix show series on YouTube under the name Jayface Radio. New episodes are usually released every 1-2 weeks. The show rotates between trance, house, and techno, with each episode typically focusing on one of these genres. The mixes are curated to take listeners on a journey, blending different styles and moods within the electronic music spectrum.

Promo Submission: If you’d like to have your music featured in the mix show - either your own tracks as an artist, or releases from your label - feel free to send a promo. Accepted genres: progressive trance and uplifting trance, melodic techno and melodic house, peaktime and hard techno. Please include your artist name, label name, and release date along with the private, downloadable SoundCloud promo URL. Do not attach audio files directly to the email.
