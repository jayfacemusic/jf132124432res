About & Discography

Jayface (Akos Juhasz) is a Hungarian electronic dance music producer and DJ, working across various genres including trance, house, techno, and electro. Jayface Music label owner, creative director and Jayface Radio show founder. It's been 20 years since I created the name Jayface back in 2005 and started writing music and registering it under that name. To celebrate the 20th anniversary of the name’s creation, I’ve launched a dedicated merch subsite under this main domain, called Jayface Shop. Albums: Drifting Worlds Album (2025), Meet Her At The Club Album (2023), The Album (2022), Summer Trance Dreams Album (2009). Singles 2025: Reflection, Cotton Candy Clouds Remix, The Life, There Is No Winter Without Snow. Singles 2024: Worlds, Ashes In The Wind Remix, Pure Love Remix, Drift, Back To The Desert, The Green Valley, The Tree Of Life: Beginnings. Singles 2023: Splash, Ashes and Roses, Plastic Human, Spoon and Sugar, Pure Love, Love is a Strange Thing, Animal Instinct, Meet Her At The Club, Follow The Love, Forgotten Life. I launched my mix show called Jayface Radio. Singles 2021-2022: Two Way, Cirrocomulus, Ashes In The Wind, Illusions, Catapult, Cotton Candy Clouds, Touch The LED, In The Desert, Strange Love, Androids, Altocomulus, Stratocomulus, Rush, Twisted Games, One Moment In Zero, Glacier, Emotions, Ice Cold, Cirruscomulus, Find Your Love. 2020-2021, different releases from different labels. 2008-2010, various releases, singels, EPs. 2007, first official release, Eye EP. 2000-2006, various music projects.
