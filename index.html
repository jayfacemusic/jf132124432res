About and discography

Jayface (Akos Juhasz) is an independent electronic music producer and DJ, working across various genres including trance, house, and techno. He is the founder, owner, and creative director of the independent record label Jayface Music, as well as the curator and DJ of the Jayface Radio show. His released albums so far: Drifting Worlds Album (2025), Meet Her At The Club Album (2023), The Album (2022), and Summer Trance Dreams Album (2009). It's been 20 years since he created the name Jayface back in 2005 and started writing music and registering it under that name. The name and logo represent a unified creative identity - artist, label, mix show, and fashion brand in one. To celebrate the 20th anniversary of the name’s creation, he also launched a dedicated merchandise subsite called Jayface Shop. From producing, mixing, and mastering tracks to designing artwork and shaping the label’s direction, the process reflects a unified creative vision throughout. Every released track, visual identity, and brand element reflects his vision and artistic integrity - executed with precision and purpose. Jayface’s music has achieved significant international recognition, with the single Let Go reaching number 1 on the Mexico iTunes trance chart, top 5 positions in India’s dance charts, and top 8 in the United States trance chart. Multiple tracks have appeared on prominent Beatport trance compilations, including The Green Valley and The Tree Of Life: Beginnings, featured in the Closing and After Hours Essentials 2024 playlists. Additionally, Jayface’s tracks have been played on various global radio shows, notably Solarstone’s Pure Trance Radio, where his work has received special acclaim - as Solarstone himself described, "This fabulous uplifter was an ID in Solarstone's sets for months, but is finally yours to have!"
