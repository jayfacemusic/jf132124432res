Promo and Guest Mix submissions

Promo Submission: If you’d like to have your music featured in the mix show - either your own tracks as an artist, releases from your label, or through your promo management - feel free to send promos. Accepted genres: progressive trance and uplifting trance, melodic techno and melodic house, progressive house, peak time and hard techno. Please include your artist name, label name, and release date along with the private, downloadable Dropbox or SoundCloud promo URL. Do not attach audio files directly to the email.

Guest Mix Submission: If you’d like to submit a guest mix for Jayface Radio, please make sure it’s your own, high quality, 1 hour long (no longer), minimum 320 kbps MP3 or WAV format, and free from clipping, distortion, or jingles. Submissions should include an artist name, a full tracklist with timestamps and label information, and links to social media profiles. The series focuses on progressive and uplifting trance, melodic techno and house, as well as hard and peak time techno. Do not attach audio files directly to the email.

(Note: Depending on the scheduling of the mixes, it may take approximately 1 week to 1 month for the promos to be included in one of the episodes. The mixes are available on YouTube as non-monetized content.)
